# What is AI Slop?

AI slop refers to low-quality, AI-generated content that:

- Contains fabricated facts
- Cites other AI-generated sources as "evidence"
- Uses polished production to appear credible
- Spreads faster than fact-checkers can respond

---

## The Problem

- 9 of top 100 fastest-growing YouTube channels publish AI slop
- AI-generated articles cite other AI articles (echo chamber)
- Users can't distinguish quality content from misinformation

---

## Our Solution

SlopScore.ai breaks this cycle by:

- Verifying claims against authoritative sources
- Tracing citation chains 2-3 levels deep
- Flagging AI-generated sources
